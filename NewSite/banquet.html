Banquets

We accept reservations of all sizes and have a banquet room that facilitates upto 75 people. For larger parties, the Starboard Side (bar room) can also be reserved for groups up to 100 people. Both rooms are greate for rehearsal dinners, class or family reunions, birthdays, Christmas parties, anniversaries, or any other gathering you may wish!


For parties of 30 or less, ordering off the regular menu is available.


If you have questions, feel free to call us at (260) 724-8181


Note: Tax and Tip is included with all prices.
